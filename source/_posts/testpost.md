## Introduction
This Project is mainly about development of a video recording system based on the ARM platform. It mainly consists of a controller, a monitor, and a camera. The main operation is done through the touch panel display. Its main functions: 
- video recording
- playback
- screenshot
- storage
- realtime review 
## Contribution
- Compiled the system design scheme and planned the functional modules according to the product requirements.
- Finished the software program of video storage and playback module with C++ independently and transplanted the algorithm to the ARM platform.
- Designed test cases, did the unit tests, and tested the whole machine.
- Contributed to the design document and user guidance.
- Acts as a team leader and coordinates the distribution of tasks among team members.
