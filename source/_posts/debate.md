## Introduction
Our debating team is the representative team of the School of Electrical Engineering at Southwest Jiaotong University. We have nearly 30 active members on our team. Our team not only represents the college in competitions, but also seeks to promote debate by organizing major debate tournaments and events within the college.
## Contribution
- Hosted and planned over 30 competitions and events such as college debate competitions, freshman meetings and appealed over 300 people to participate cumulatively.
- Provided more than 10 debate skills training and competition guidance workshops to members. Helped the team win over 10 school-level debate competitions.
- Organized team meetings by communicating with team members and leading meetings.
