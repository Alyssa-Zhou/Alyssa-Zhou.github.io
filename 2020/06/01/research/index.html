Research Training Program - Development of Automatic Train Driving Device Based on Safety Computer Platform

Category: Research

Introduction

The goal of the project is to develop an automated train driving device. I worked with a team of Train Control & Traction Drive Lab at Southwest Jiaotong University.

Contribution

  • Contributed to the physical model of the train and analyzed its performance.
  • Completed the MatLab program to simulate the model we built, and found the parameters that make the model work best.
  • Helped to install the automatic train driving device and tested it.
  • Participate in the preparation of related papers which was helpful to the successful conclusion of the research.
